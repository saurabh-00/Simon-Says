INSTRUCTIONS:

  • Press any key to start the Game.
  • After pressing key, any one button will flash on your screen.
  • Press the same button that flashed. You have to follow sequence, according to game.
  • If your sequence will be correct, level will get updated.
  • Once you press wrong button, Game will be over.
  • You can restart game again by pressing any key again.
